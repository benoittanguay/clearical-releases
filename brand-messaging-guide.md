## Brand Foundation

### What Clearical Is

**Clearical** is an automatic time tracking application that captures work passively while professionals stay in flow. It uses AI to understand context, suggests the right projects, and syncs seamlessly with Jira and Tempo for enterprise teams.

### The Problem We Solve

Time tracking is broken. Knowledge workers face an impossible choice:

1. **Track meticulously** ‚Üí Constant interruptions destroy focus and flow
2. **Track at end of day** ‚Üí Inaccurate reconstruction, lost billable hours
3. **Don't track at all** ‚Üí Revenue leakage, no project visibility

The average professional loses 15-30 minutes per day to time tracking friction. For a team of 10, that's 50+ hours per month of lost productivity‚Äîspent on the very tool meant to measure productivity.

### Our Solution

Clearical eliminates this choice. It runs silently in the background, capturing work context through:
- Screenshot analysis (AI-powered, privacy-first)
- Window and application monitoring
- Meeting detection and transcription
- Calendar integration

Then it intelligently suggests the right bucket or Jira issue. One click logs to Tempo. No timers. No end-of-week panic. No friction.

### Mission Statement

> **We believe your best work happens when you're not thinking about time.**
>
> Clearical exists to eliminate the friction between doing great work and getting credit for it. We're building a future where time documentation is invisible, accurate, and automatic‚Äîso professionals can stay in flow and organizations can make better decisions.

### Vision

A world where no one loses billable hours to forgotten work, and no one breaks focus to track time.

### Writing Principles

**1. Lead with the benefit, not the feature**
- No: "AI-powered screenshot analysis"
- Yes: "Know exactly what you worked on without remembering"

**2. Respect the reader's time**
- Headlines: 6-12 words maximum
- Body paragraphs: 2-3 sentences
- Bullet points for scanning

**3. Be specific over generic**
- No: "Save time on time tracking"
- Yes: "Log a full week to Tempo in under 60 seconds"

**4. Show, don't tell**
- No: "Easy to use"
- Yes: "One click from captured time to logged timesheet"

**5. Acknowledge the pain before presenting the solution**
- No: "Clearical automatically tracks your time"
- Yes: "Reconstructing your week from memory is exhausting. Clearical captures it as you work."

## Language Guidelines

### Words & Phrases We Use

| Category | Preferred Terms |
|----------|-----------------|
| **Action** | Capture, document, sync, log, attribute |
| **Benefit** | Flow, focus, effortless, automatic, seamless |
| **Value** | Reclaim, recover, protect, accurate |
| **Technical** | On-device, local, private, native |
| **Tone** | Smart, intelligent, thoughtful |

### Words & Phrases We Avoid

| Avoid | Why | Alternative |
|-------|-----|-------------|
| Track/tracking | Surveillance connotation | Capture, document |
| Monitor, watch | Too invasive | Understand, learn |
| Spy, surveillance | Obviously negative | ‚Äî |
| Productivity hacks | Hustle culture | Workflow, process |
| Synergy, leverage | Corporate jargon | Work together, use |
| Cutting-edge, innovative | Overused, meaningless | Smart, thoughtful |
| Easy, simple | Subjective, often untrue | Effortless, one-click |
| Revolutionary | Hyperbolic | Different, new approach |

### Formatting Standards

**Numbers:**
- Spell out one through nine
- Use numerals for 10+
- Always use numerals for stats: "94% reduction" not "ninety-four percent"
- Use "$" for money: "$4,200" not "4,200 dollars"

**Punctuation:**
- Oxford comma: Always use
- Em dashes: For emphasis‚Äîlike this
- Exclamation points: Maximum one per page/email

**Capitalization:**
- Headlines: Sentence case ("Time tracking that respects your time")
- Product name: "Clearical" (always capitalized)
- Features: Lowercase unless proper noun ("automatic capture" not "Automatic Capture")
